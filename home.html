About Kiwi-X
Kiwi-X branded products represent an ever increasing range of quality, affordable aftermarket hardparts available for motorcycles and ATV’s. Constant development and testing is undertaken to ensure ongoing performance and suitability for New Zealand’s harsh climate. R2 Enterprises works with major o.e.m factories in Japan and Asia to bring you high grade components without the high cost associated with genuine parts replacement. In many areas, design improvements have been introduced to enhance the longevity of selected product lines.
